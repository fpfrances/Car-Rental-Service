About Knights Auto Car Rental

Knights Auto Car Rental was founded in the heart of the United States with a passion for providing exceptional service and a seamless experience for all our customers. Established in 2002, our journey began with a simple idea: to redefine the way people travel by offering reliable, affordable, and convenient car rental solutions.

Our founders, a group of seasoned professionals in the automotive industry, recognized the need for a rental service that prioritized customer satisfaction above all else. With a commitment to excellence and a vision to become a trusted name in the car rental industry, Knights Auto Car Rental was born.

Headquartered in Orlando, Florida, our strategic location allows us to serve customers from coast to coast. Whether you're exploring the bustling streets of New York City, embarking on a scenic road trip through the mountains of Colorado, or soaking up the sun in sunny California, Knights Auto Car Rental is here to make your journey unforgettable.

At Knights Auto, we pride ourselves on our extensive fleet of vehicles, ranging from sleek sedans to spacious SUVs and everything in between. Each vehicle in our fleet undergoes rigorous inspection and maintenance to ensure optimal performance and safety for our customers.

But what truly sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Our dedicated team of professionals works tirelessly to meet and exceed the expectations of every customer, providing personalized service and assistance every step of the way.

Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, solo or with family and friends, Knights Auto Car Rental is your trusted partner for all your car rental needs. Experience the difference with Knights Auto and let us turn your travel dreams into reality.

Thank you for choosing Knights Auto Car Rental. We look forward to serving you on your next adventure!
